# Privacy Policy for Zakat Pro

**Last Updated: February 02, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Zakat Pro**. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you may provide while using our Service. This application is designed with a **"Privacy First"** approach. The Service is provided "as-is," and we ensure that your spiritual and financial data remains entirely under your control.

## 2. Information Collection and Use
We believe in absolute data privacy.

### A. Automatic Data Collection
**Zakat Pro** does **NOT** automatically collect, store, or share any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) or sensitive user data. This includes, but is not limited to:
* Name, Phone Number, or Address.
* Precise or coarse Location Data.
* Device Identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, etc.).
* Contact lists or personal photos.

All Zakat calculations, asset details, and distribution records are processed and stored **locally on your device**. No data is sent to our servers.

### B. In-App Donations (Google Play Billing)
The app offers an optional donation feature to support the project.
* All financial transactions are handled securely by **Google Play Billing**.
* We **do not** collect or store your credit card numbers, bank account details, or any other financial credentials.
* Google provides us only with a confirmation of the purchase to fulfill the donation request.

### C. Voluntary Communication (Contact Support)
If you choose to contact us via email for support, feedback, or suggestions, we will receive your email address and the content of your message.
* This action is strictly voluntary and initiated by you.
* We use your email address solely for the purpose of replying to your query.
* We do not store your email in any marketing database or share it with third parties.

## 3. Local Data Storage & Permissions
To provide a premium offline experience, the app stores certain data locally on your device (using **Room Database** & **SharedPreferences**). This includes:
* **Zakat History:** Past calculation records and dates.
* **Zakat Diary:** Personal logs of to whom and when Zakat was distributed.
* **Preferences:** Custom Nisab prices (Gold/Silver), currency symbols, and theme settings.
* **Storage Access:** The app requires storage permission strictly to save and export the **PDF Report** of your calculations to your device when you choose to do so.

**Note:** This data is stored strictly within the app's private directory on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any third party.

## 4. Offline Functionality & Third-Party Services
**Zakat Pro** is designed as an offline-first application.
* **No Internet Required:** The core functions of calculating Zakat, managing history, and reading Islamic rules do NOT require an active internet connection.
* **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics), advertising SDKs, or any tracking pixels.
* **Limited Connectivity:** Internet is only required for the In-App Donation process via Google Play Store.

## 5. Data Security
Since we do not collect or transmit your sensitive Zakat data over the internet, your information is safe from remote hacking or server-side data breaches. The security of your data depends on the security of your physical device.

## 6. User Control & Data Deletion
You have total control over your data:
* **Clearing Data:** You can reset all saved settings and history by going to your device's **Settings > Apps > Zakat Pro > Storage > Clear Data**.
* **Uninstalling:** Uninstalling the app will automatically and permanently delete all locally stored calculation history, settings, and diary entries.
